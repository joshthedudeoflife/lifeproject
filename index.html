Joshua Chou's introduction

Lets talk about risk. Its something all of us here are familiar with. A little over a year ago I decided to leave the safe world of accounting, learn iOS and Ruby on Rails, Ive sold everything you see behind me, bought a one way plane ticket here from Kentucky, all because I want to build cool stuff 40 years ago my grandfather sold everything, left Taiwan to go to Argentina without speaking a word of Spanish and started the first super market chain in Buenos Aires. What he taught me is that a life without risk is not a life worth living. At one point all of you had to leave a safe world behind and take a risk because you wanted to build something cool. So lets build something cool together. Lets throw away the rule book and fall into the bliss. Im all in. Im here, with nothing more than a bag and a dream (and an amazing girl friend) and Im not going back. My name is Joshua Chou and Im a web developer.
